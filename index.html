About Us

The ML-Health Research Group focuses on advancing healthcare through machine learning, with a particular emphasis on neurodegenerative diseases such as ALS and Parkinson's. We aim to bridge the gap between computational science and clinical applications, driving innovation in diagnosis and personalised treatment.

Aims and Scope

The ML-Health Research Group focuses on leveraging cutting-edge technologies to advance healthcare and improve the lives of individuals with neurological and other medical conditions. Our mission is to develop innovative solutions for early diagnosis, personalised treatment, and rehabilitation through a multidisciplinary approach that bridges machine learning, biomedical engineering, and clinical sciences.

  • 1. Machine Learning and AI: Development and application of advanced machine learning models for analysing neuroimaging, sensor data, and clinical datasets to identify biomarkers and predict disease progression.
  • 2. Sensors and Wearable Technology: Exploration of sensors and wearable devices for capturing real-time patient data, focusing on motor deficiencies, rehabilitation, and disease monitoring.
  • 3. Prosthetics and Assistive Devices: Design and integration of engineering solutions, including prosthetics and robotic devices, to support patients with motor impairments and improve quality of life.
  • 4. Biomedical Engineering Innovations: Collaborative development of novel diagnostic tools and therapeutic technologies in collaboration with clinicians and industry partners.
  • 5. Clinical and Interdisciplinary Research: Strong collaboration with clinicians to ensure that research outcomes translate into real-world impact, improving healthcare systems and patient outcomes globally.
