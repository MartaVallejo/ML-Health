About Us

The ML-Health Research Group focuses on advancing healthcare through machine learning, with a particular emphasis on neurodegenerative diseases such as ALS and Parkinson's. We aim to bridge the gap between computational science and clinical applications, driving innovation in diagnosis and personalised treatment.
