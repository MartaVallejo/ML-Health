Group Lead

Dr Marta Vallejo

Assistant Professor at Heriot-Watt University and Head of the ML-Health Research Group. Dr Vallejo is an expert in applying machine learning to neurodegenerative diseases, particularly ALS and Parkinson's, for diagnosis and personalised treatment.
